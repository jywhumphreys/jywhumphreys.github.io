My Background
With over 5 years of diverse engineering and leadership experience, I have honed my skills in design, testing, and project management through involvement in competitive student teams, personal projects, and professional roles.
I am currently involved with UCLA’s Formula SAE team, where I lead the Controls subteam, and am also the Responsible Engineer for the Brakes & Pedalbox subsystem. Before university, I was heavily involved in the FIRST Robotics Competition, founding the first community team serving our area and leading the Electronics subteam.
Beyond my technical pursuits, I am an Eagle Scout and enjoy working on automotive projects in my free time. I am always eager to expand my knowledge and take on new challenges.
